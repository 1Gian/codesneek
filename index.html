What is VPS Hosting?

VPS hosting stands for Virtual Private Server. Which means that the virtualized server’s resources are totally at your disposal and use, unlike shared hosting in which you had to share the resources with other people on the shared server. Keep in mind that VPS is different from a dedicated server as it is developed under system virtualization. System virtualization means that the server has many virtual machines running on it and the system resources which mean the RAM, CPU, disk storage has been partitioned between these virtual machines. Why do you need it? If you have a website with high traffic and the shared servers are not able to manage that kind of traffic, your best bet is now on VPS servers. VPS servers not only come cheap, but they can also handle a decent amount of traffic.
